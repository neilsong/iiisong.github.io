McDonald's Week Info


McDonalds Week is an annual St. Mark’s event that aims to raise money for Austin Street Shelter. This fundraiser will take place at St. Mark’s from March 1–4. With help from McDonalds and several Preston Royal restaurants, we hope get students involved with this event and ultimately support Austin Street Shelter. Additionally, all throughout this week, we will have morning events including a rap battle, chemistry show, and many more. You can also donate by buying raffle tickets and t-shirts during this week. In these trying times, we are honored to partner with the many organizations involved in the event, and we look forward to a successful week!

Events for this Week

Monday:
Ping Pong Tournament

It’s a 4 man bracket. The first round will be played to 11, win by 2, switching serves every three. The final round will be played to 21, win by 2, switching serves every five. Sucker serve will be used, and the man down will have 3 chances on the serve per point.

First round will be:
Freshman CJ Ness vs Sophomore Shreyan Daulat and Junior Ekansh Tambe vs Senior Luke Annett.

Tuesday:
Rap Battle

Freshman Adam Kappleman will take on veteran rapper, Senior Aaryan Puri. Sophomore Sal Hussain will take on Junior Thomas Philip. The crowd will vote on who’s rap they liked best and the winners will battle it out in the next round. Each rap will have some tie in to Ghostbusters, and if ghostbusters is not mentioned, it is an automatic DQ for that contestant.

Wednesday:
Relay Race

The race will consist of 5 parts that will be announced and explained prior to the race Wednesday morning.

Freshmans: Parker Steinbruek, Clark Cooper, Raja Mehendale, and Bryan Graham
Sophomores: Adam Alfalahi, Silas Holser, and Patrick Flanagan
Juniors: Elijah Ellis, Thomas Philip, Sahil Dodda, and Toby Barrett
Seniors: Blake Hudspeth, Benny Wang, Jackson Fair, and Sam Morgan

Thursday:
The Annual Chemistry Show from Mr. Owens

Mr. Owens always puts on a cool show with many experiments and demonstrations.
