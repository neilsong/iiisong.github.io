Monday:  Ping Pong Tournament

It’s a 4 man bracket. The first round will be played to 11, win by 2, switching serves every three. The final round will be played to 21, win by 2, switching serves every five. Sucker serve will be used, and the man down will have 3 chances on the serve per point.

First round will be:
Freshman CJ Ness vs Sophomore Shreyan Daulat
Junior Ekansh Tambe vs Senior Luke Annett.

Tuesday:  Rap Battle

Freshman Adam Kappleman will take on veteran rapper, Senior Aaryan Puri. Sophomore Sal Hussain will take on Junior Thomas Philip. The crowd will vote on who’s rap they liked best and the winners will battle it out in the next round. Each rap will have some tie in to Ghostbusters, and if ghostbusters is not mentioned, it is an automatic DQ for that contestant.
